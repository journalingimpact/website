About Us

Journaling Impact is a passion project that has been started by two individuals enamored by the process of research. Ronak and Vidya know each other from the last ten years and have been encouraging each other to push themselves towards excellence. Journaling Impact is an outcome of the innumerable, cognitively stimulating discussions that the two have engaged in. 

Through this project, we aim to break the barriers that is often associated with the term 'research'. We aim to achieve this by exploring research through areas of our interests, by putting out our thoughts when we pursue a course or read an academic article. We will note down what we feel about the topic and what we can do about it. We want to encourage readers to think and question. We want each one to reflect on the content they read/ listen/ watch, and not remain passive about matters of their interest.
